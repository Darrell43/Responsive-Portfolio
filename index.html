About Me

I am a proud graduate of Rutgers University with a degree in Information Technology and Informatics and a minor in Human Resource & Management. My experience working hands on with Sakai developers and instructional designers has made me well versed in many aspects of educational technology.

Within my support role, I use my communication skills to complete daily tasks in a friendly and timely manner. I use my expertise to provide Help Desk support for Sakai, Canvas, eCollege, and Moodle. Prior to user support, I worked with Rutgers Recreation assisting students, faculty, staff, and guests navigating different areas of the building and setting up intramural activities for the College Ave Gym.
